Hands on indicates - this subject category is having additional fees. Hands on may be lab or lecture units. The additional fees is added to total amount of the subject.
For example Computer Sc 1 tution fees is 6*250(per load) = 1500.00 and hands amount is 600.00.
Total tution fee for Computer Sc 1 is 2100.00.

 

 

 

 

PinCode protection:
   Pincode protection is an added level of protection to usual login method. School administration is having option whether to apply pincode protection. With pincode protection, Key loggers fail to record complete login information required to login to SchoolBLIZ system.
   For the first time log on, users set a 4 digits pincode after entering user id and password. If user forgets pincode, system admin must resets password to reset pincode and password.
