DORMITORY HELP

Maximum capacity of room
This indicates maximum no of occupants can share a room. If a room is taken by a family/staff or a student does not want to share, even though the room capacity is more than 1, room will not be shared by anyone else.
All the expenses(rental/electricity/water bill and other charges) for the room will be posted to the no of occupants.

Total Occupants as of today
This indicates total no of Occupants for the selected room as of this day. This can't be more than maximum capacity of room. Room status is OCCUPIED once the room reached maximum no of occupant capacity.

Remaining no of Occupants
This indicates no of occupants can still occupy in same room. This value equals to (total capacity - total occupant)

Occupancy status This can have two values.
1. Occupying partially : If the occupant wants to share the room with other occupants until the maximum capacity reaches. The charges posted is devided among the occupants.
2. Occupying complete room : If the occupant does not want to share the room with other occupant. In such cases the room status is set to occupied once occupied by the occupant. Only one occupant is responsible for all the charges posted for this room (rental,water bill,electricity,other charges etc.)
