SALARY RATE

View/ Search Employee Salary Rate
 

Encode employee's salary rate
Allow users to manage salary information of employees.

Field Definition:

- Hyper Link “FIRST PREVIOUS NEXT LAST” - this will allows user to move to the next employee without using the search button or typing the employee ID.

- Effectivity Date - inclusive dates when the particular salary rate is effective. For unknown duration of the salary rate the effective date to field may be left blank.

- Salary Period Type – indicates when a salary is given to the employee.

- Basic Rate - field where user can specify the basic rate of an employee.

o Monthly rate – salary rate of the employee per month.

o Number of days dropdown – used in auto computation of the daily rate based on the monthly rate (Monthly rate / number of days = daily rate)

o Daily rate – salary rate of the employee on a daily basis

o Hourly rate – salary rate of the employee per hour.

o Number of hours dropdown – used in auto computation of the daily rate based on the daily rate (daily rate / number of hours = hourly rate)

- Teaching Rate – Usually used in computing the salary a faculty based on the number of hours/ load units.

- Overload rate – Rate per hour/unit when a faculty exceeds the maximum load set.

- Bank Name – Name of bank where the salary the employee is forwarded.

- Account Number – Account number of the employee in the bank selected. This will later be used in bank transmittal.

- ATM Account (Check box) – This will indicate if the employee’s back account is for ATM.
